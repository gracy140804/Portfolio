Internship WordPress Projects

Sweet Heaven – Bakery Website

WordPress Elementor WooCommerce (Basics) Responsive Design

Sweet Heaven is a delightful bakery website designed to showcase a variety of baked goods, from cakes to pastries. Built during my internship, this project involved creating a visually appealing layout that complements the bakery's brand. I used Elementor to build custom page sections, including a product gallery, a detailed contact page, and an interactive menu. The site is fully responsive, ensuring a seamless experience for users browsing on mobile or desktop.

Call Taxi – Service Website

WordPress Theme Customization Booking Form SEO Basics

Call Taxi is a professional service-oriented website developed for a local taxi business. The primary focus of this project was usability and quick access to information. I implemented a streamlined booking request system and integrated Google Maps for easy location tracking. The project helped me sharpen my skills in theme customization and plugin integration, ensuring that the final product met the client's business needs while maintaining a clean, modern aesthetic.
